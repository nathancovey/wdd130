Home Page

Welcome! My name is Nathan Covey and I'm so glad you made it here. On this site, you will find relevant information about my background, experience, and skills.

Experience

From October 2021 to July 2023 I worked as the VP of Marketing at a startup called Galvan, a blockchain-based health ecosystem that empowers users to track and get rewarded for taking care of their mental and physical health. During my time here, I planned and executed GTM strategy that generated $2,000,000 revenue in first year, grew Web3 community to 10,000+ individuals on Discord and Twitter, managed team of five marketers to achieve KPIs, and collaborated with product and engineering teams from prototype to production.

From October 2020 to October 2021, two fellow co-founders and I created a startup called HappySol, which used AI science-based technology to analyze the tones in your voice and show your levels of stress, well-being, and sleep quality. During that year, I was in charge of creating a innovative iOS/Android mental health app through strategic product development and customer research. As CEO, I also led our fundraising efforts which resulted in an acquisition by Galvan after 1 year of operations.

From July 2017 until the present day, I have worked in various positions for a charity founded by my family called Bridle Up Hope: The Rachel Covey Foundation, which is changing the lives of women and girls through equestrian-assisted learning (EAL) with The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People integrated throughout. To date, 2,000 participants have passed through our stables and 90% call it "life-changing." We have also expanded to 10+ global locations, with a vision to eventually reach 1,000. As a co-founder of the organization, I continue to assist on a monthly basis with its marketing and growth efforts. Through the years, I have developed a Facebook Ads funnel with an average 8:1 ROAS, assisted in marketing and donation campaigns that have reached $3,000,000+ in contributions to date, built the website and technology platform that supports 10+ global locations in 5 different languages, implemented strategy that has generated 200+ 5-star reviews to date, grew social media following to 10,000+ in 6 months, and launched ecommerce charity shop that generates, on average, $100,000 revenue/year.

Skills

My skills include: Digital Marketing (Funnel Wizard!), Content Creation (Video, Copy), Product Development (Figma), PPC Advertising (Google, Meta),SEO (Ahrefs, Semrush), Web Development (Webflow),Design (Canva, Adobe CC), Analytics (GA4 & GTM),Email/SMS Campaigns, Organized and Detail-Oriented, Deep Web3 Knowledge, and Spanish (full prof. proficiency)

Education

I started college at Brigham Young University, where I received a half-tuition scholarship and was accepted into the nationally-ranked Entrepreneurship major. HappySol was acquired by Galvan while my time there, at which time I made the difficult decision to work full time and transfer to an online school.

The school I transferred to is Brigham Young University-Idaho, where I will graduate in April 2024 with a Bachelor's of Science in Applied Business management.

Volunteering

From July 2018 to April 2020, I was a full-time volunteer missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints for two years in Buenos Aires, Argentina. During these two years, I volunteered 70 hours a week, preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ and serving those in need, learned fluent Spanish and presented workshops/trainings on a weekly basis, immersed myself in various LATAM cultures (Argentine, Chilean, Peruvian, etc.), and led a church organization of 200 young adults and improved KPIs by 30%.
