Frequently Asked Questions

What skill level is required?

At The Rapids, we accommodate all skill levels. Beginners can enjoy our gentle Class I-II river tours, which provide a scenic experience with a touch of adventure. For thrill-seekers or those with some rafting experience, our Class III-IV rapids offer an exciting and challenging adventure. Safety briefings and equipment are provided to ensure a great experience for everyone.

What should I bring for my rafting trip?

For a trip with The Rapids, we recommend that you bring quick-drying clothes, secure shoes that can get wet, sunscreen, a hat, and sunglasses with a retention strap. We also advise bringing a change of clothes and a towel for after the trip. Personal flotation devices and safety gear will be provided on-site.

How long are the trips?

Our rafting adventures vary in length to provide the best experience for our guests. Half-day trips typically last about 2-3 hours on the water, while full-day excursions can last about 4-6 hours, which includes a lunch break on the riverbank. For those wanting a more extended experience, we offer multi-day rafting adventures that include camping.

Is there an age limit for river rafting with The Rapids?

Yes, for safety reasons, we have age restrictions. For our Class I-II river trips, participants must be at least 5 years old. For the more intense Class III-IV rapids, participants must be at least 12 years old. All minors must be accompanied by an adult. Our goal is to ensure a safe and enjoyable adventure for all our guests.
